7.3 Update Views in SQL

Enter data into the ttXXGLAccounts_XXGLAccounts_Upload table.

To update the view:

  1. Open Management Studio on the Desktop.
  2. Enter SQL Server Name from the credentials provided at the beginning of class.
  3. Enter Login and Password from the credentials provided at the beginning of class.
  4. Expand to the sdbNorthwindXX database.
  5. Expand Tables.
  6. Right click on the ttXXGLAccounts_XX_GLAccounts_Upload table.
  7. Select Edit Top Rows.
  8. Open the Integrate Exercises Spreadsheet on the Desktop.
  9. Copy the data not the Header on the Upload Table Worksheet.
  10. Paste the data in the ttXXGLAccounts_XX_GLAccounts_Upload table in SQL.
  11. Right click on the ttXXGLAccounts_XXGLAccounts_Loop1_1_Upload table.
  12. Select Edit Top Rows.
  13. Return to the Integrate Exercises Spreadsheet on the Desktop.
  14. Enter the data from the Loop Table Worksheet in the ttXXGLAccounts_XXGLAccounts_ Loop1_1_Upload table in SQL.

Modify the txXXGLAccounts_XXGLAccounts_Int view.

To modify the view to format todays date:

  1. Open the txXXGLAccounts_XXGLAccounts_Int from sdbNorthwindXX, in design view.
  2. Copy [ACGL_HEAD-BLDAT].
  3. Enter CONVERT (varchar(10), GETDATE(), 101) in the Colum Field.
  4. Paste [ACGL_HEAD-BLDAT] in the Alias field.
  5. Copy [ACGL_HEAD-BUDAT].
  6. Enter CONVERT (varchar(10), GETDATE(), 101) in the Colum Field.
  7. Paste [ACGL_HEAD-BUDAT] in the Alias field.
  8. Click Save.
