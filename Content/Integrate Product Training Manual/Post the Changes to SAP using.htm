8.1 Post the Changes to SAP using BDC Recording

To activate and post changes:

  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  2. Locate XXGLAccounts Process.
  3. Click Activate/Deactivate to activate the Process.
  4. Click Post .
  5. Click Post in the Dependent pane.
  6. Click OK, a message displays.

  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  2. Click Messages .

  1. Make note of the Document number.
  2. Return to SQL.
  3. Expand dgSAP database.
  4. Edit Top Rows for BKPF table.
  5. Press Ctrl and 2 on the keyboard to Show Criteria Pane.
  6. Enter the Document number preceded by 0 in the Filter for BELNR.
  7. Enter todays date in this format, YYYYMMDD, in the Filter for BLDAT.
  8. Execute to view the record posted.
