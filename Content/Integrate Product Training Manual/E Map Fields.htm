7.4 E. Map Fields

Fields are automatically mapped when the tables and views were generated in Integrate.

To map the fields

  1. Return to DSP.
  2. Click Field Mappings for txXXGLAccounts_XXGLaccounts_Int, the fields display in the Dependent pane.
  3. Verify the fields are mapped.
  4. Click Field Mappings for txXXGLAccounts_XXGLaccounts_Loop1_1Int, the fields display in the Dependent pane.
  5. Click the Browser Back button.
