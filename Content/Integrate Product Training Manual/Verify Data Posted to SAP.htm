1.5 Verify Data Posted to SAP

To verify that records were posted:

  1. Open SAP GUI.
  1. Enter trainingXX in User field.
  1. Enter trainingXX in Password field.
  1. Click Ok .
  1. Enter SE16 transaction code.
  1. Click Enter.
  1. Enter MARA in Table Name field.
  1. Click Enter.
  1. Enter the first material number from the Messages column, and the last material number from the message column in the MATNR fields.

Material Numbers

  1. Click Execute .
  1. Make a note of the first Material Number, it will be needed in a future exercise.

 

NOTE: The posted records that displayed on the Message page in Integrate display on the Data Browser:    Table [table name] Select Entries screen in SAP.
