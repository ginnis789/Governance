6.3 Modify Template

The template created, in Exercise 6.0, is missing a field required in SAP to post successfully. Modify the template to add the MG03STEUER-TAXKM field to screen number 4200. The template then will contain all necessary fields to successfully extend semi-finished materials to an existing plant.

To modify a template:

  1. Click Categories on the Navigation pane.
  2. Locate TrainingXX Category.
  1. Click Templates .
  1. Locate XXExtendToOrgUnits Template.
  2. Click Process for the Template.
  3. Click Deactivate All , a message displays.

NOTE:The Deactivate All icon deactivates the status of all processes associated with the template as well as the template.

  1. Click the Ok button.
  2. Click the Browser Back button.
  3. Click Vertical View .
  4. Click Configuration tab.
  5. Click BDC Screen .
  6. Highlight Priority 50 Screen-Program 4200 – SAPLMGMM in the BDC Screen pane, the fields display in the Dependent pane.

BDC Screens

  1. Click the Add button in the Dependent pane.
  2. Enter 60 in Priority field.
  3. Enter MG03STEUER-TAXKM(03) in Field Name field.
  4. Enter 1 in the Field Value field.
  5. Click the Save button.

 

BDC Screen Fields

  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  2. Close the Vertical View.
  3. Click Activate/Deactivate for the Template to activate.
  4. Click the OK button.
  5. Click the Browser Back button.
  6. Click Processes .
  7. Locate the XXExtendToOrgUnits rocess.
  8. Click Loops in Dependent pane for the Process.

NOTE:MG03STEUER-TAXKM should be mapped in the Template. If it is not, edit the record and select TAXKM from the Mapping Value list box.

Mapped Field

  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  2. Click Activate/Deactivate for the Process to activate.
  3. Click the OK button.
  4. Click Post for the XXExtendToOrgUnits Process.
  5. Click Post for the Process Post in the Dependent pane.

NOTE:Message returns that all records extended successfully, however, the BOASessionCreate BDC Posting Method is still selected on the Process Post. A new SM35 session was created to test the accuracy of the Template.

  1. Click OK .
