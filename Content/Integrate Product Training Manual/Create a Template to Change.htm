3.0 Create a Template to Change Weight Unit

Create a template to change the Weight Unit.

To create a change material template:

  1. Click Categories on the Navigation pane.
  1. Locate TrainingXX Category.
  1. Click Templates .
  1. Click the Add button.
  1. Enter XXChangeWeightUnit in Template Name field.
  1. Enter Change Weight Unit in Description field.
  1. Select BDC Script from Type list box.
  1. Select SAP from Connection ID list box.

NOTE:The Connection ID is the connection to the business system, in this case, the SAP instance in which the recording is done in. Integrate can connect to multiple SAP instances which allows recordings to be completed in one and posting in another.

  1. Click the Save button, the Vertical View displays.
  1. Enter MM02 in Transaction Code list box.
  1. Click the Save button.
  1. Click Record , a message displays.
  1. Click the OK button.
  1. Enter SAP credentials if prompted.

NOTE:If the SAP screen does not display immediately, it may be hidden behind the window.

  1. Enter the First Number from the messages in Exercise 1.4 in Material Field.
  1. Click Enter on the Toolbar, the Select View screen displays.
  1. Select Basic Data 1.

NOTE:This purpose of this training is about how to use the application, not about how best to perform a recording in SAP. The recordings used for training are simple and therefore do not necessarily reflect the challenges that may arise when designing templates to post more complete SAP actions.

  1. Click .
  2. Locate Ext. Matl Group.
  1. Enter 01 in Ext. Matl Group field to overwrite the value.
  1. Locate Weight Unit.
  1. Enter LB in Weight Unit field to overwrite the value.
  2. Click Save , a Messages displays in DSP.
  3. Click Vertical View for the XXChangeWeightUnit template.
  4. Click Configuration Tab.
  5. Click BDC Screen to view the recorded screens and fields.
  6. Click the Browser Back button.
  7. Close the Vertical View.
  8. Click Activate/Deactivate , a message displays.
  9. Click OK to confirm.
